About Me

Hi, my name is Ronniel. A UX/UI designer and Front-end developer from Philippines. I have a great passion with design and creativity is one of the aspect that I explore. Design doesn't need to be complicated, and I simplify this based on the user experience. I am a Quadriplegic designer, and yes! I don't have a functional 10 fingers and a full time bedridden. Most of the time, I spent my days building websites, do graphic designs and wireframing.

I have 2 Cats, and their name is Serif & Palette. Yes, I derived their names from Graphic Design. See how passionate I am in this field?
